Programs Details

Together For Orphans offers a range of programs designed to provide holistic support for young orphans, focusing on their growth and development. These programs aim to empower children to thrive and build a brighter future.

Scholarship for Future

Provides financial aid for orphans to ensure they complete their basic and secondary education, helping them prepare for higher education or entry into the workforce.

Program Features

  • Secondary Education Scholarship Full or partial scholarships to cover tuition, uniforms, books, and other school-related expenses, ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent orphans from completing their education.
  • National Exam Preparation Classes Offers specialized tutoring and exam preparation to help students succeed in national exams, which are critical for advancing to higher education or securing scholarships for tertiary education.
  • Tutoring Support Provides additional academic support outside of school hours through tutoring sessions in core subjects like mathematics, science, and language, enhancing students' academic performance.

Pathway to Career

This program is designed to equip individuals with practical skills needed to excel in their careers. It focuses on vocational training and skill-building workshops relevant to various industries.

Program Features

  • Individual Career Counseling Provides personalized career guidance to help participants explore different career paths, set achievable goals, and understand the steps needed to pursue their chosen fields.
  • Self-Development Workshops Focuses on developing soft skills like communication, leadership, time management, and teamwork, which are essential for both academic success and professional growth.
  • Job Interview Simulation Offers mock interview sessions that simulate real-world job interviews, helping participants practice answering questions, building confidence, and receiving feedback to improve their interviewing skills.

Mental Wellbeing and Confidence Building

The Health Initiatives program is dedicated to promoting overall health and wellness within the community through a series of targeted public health initiatives and educational outreach efforts.

Program Features

  • Regular Counseling Sessions Provides regular access to professional counselors who help participants address personal challenges, cope with trauma, and build emotional resilience in a safe and supportive environment.
  • Confidence Building Workshops Delivers workshops aimed at improving self-esteem and confidence through activities like public speaking, creative expression, and positive feedback sessions, empowering orphans to feel more secure in themselves.
  • Mindfulness and Stress Management Training Teaches mindfulness techniques and stress management strategies to help orphans manage anxiety, cope with pressures, and maintain emotional balance, which is crucial for overall mental wellbeing.
